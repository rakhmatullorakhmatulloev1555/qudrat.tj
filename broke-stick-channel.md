# Broke Stick — YouTube Channel Package

## BANNER PROMPT (генерировать 2560x1440, важное — в центре)

Extremely simple amateur MS Paint style drawing, as if drawn quickly by hand by
someone who cannot draw. Wide horizontal YouTube channel banner. Clean white
background, thick uneven shaky black outlines, flat colors only, no shading, no
gradients, no 3D, no professional vector art.

In the exact center: large hand-written text "BROKE STICK" in bold wobbly black
letters, perfectly readable and spelled correctly. Directly under it, smaller
hand-written text: "money stories, badly drawn".

To the left of the text: one stick figure standing next to a crashing chart arrow
pointing down, empty pockets turned out, with a sad face. To the right of the text:
a simple hand-drawn Eiffel Tower with a price tag on it, a lottery ticket, and a
falling dollar bill.

The far left and far right edges stay mostly empty with tiny doodles: a question
mark, a coin, a crumpled dollar bill. All key elements sit in the central horizontal
strip, away from top and bottom edges.

### Памятка по загрузке
- Генерировать 2560x1440 (16:9).
- Всё важное должно быть в центральной зоне 1546x423 — только она видна на телефонах.
- После загрузки проверить предпросмотр «ТВ / десктоп / мобильный» в YouTube Studio.
