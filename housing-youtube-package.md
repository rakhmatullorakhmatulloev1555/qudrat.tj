## DESCRIPTION

In 2005, everyone in America knew house prices only went up. A former doctor with a glass eye read the documents nobody else opened and bet over a billion dollars they were all wrong. For two years his own investors tried to stop him. Then 2008 happened.

This is the story of Michael Burry and Scion Capital: how he found the rot inside subprime mortgage bonds, convinced banks to invent the very instrument he used against them, survived two years of lawsuits and panic, and walked away with $725 million while the financial system burned.
