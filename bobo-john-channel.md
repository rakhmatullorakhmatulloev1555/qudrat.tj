# Bobo John — YouTube Channel Package

## BANNER PROMPT (генерировать 2560x1440, важное — в центре)

Extremely simple amateur MS Paint style drawing, as if drawn quickly by hand by
someone who cannot draw. Wide horizontal YouTube channel banner. Clean white
background, thick uneven shaky black outlines, flat colors only, no shading, no
gradients, no 3D, no professional vector art.

In the exact center: large hand-written text "BOBO JOHN" in bold wobbly black
letters, perfectly readable and spelled correctly. Directly under it, smaller
hand-written text: "money stories, badly drawn".

To the left of the text: one stick figure with round head and dot eyes sitting on
a pile of coins, reading a thick document. To the right of the text: a simple
hand-drawn chart with an arrow going up and then crashing down, a tiny house and
a falling dollar bill next to it.

The far left and far right edges of the banner stay almost empty, just a few tiny
doodles: a question mark, a coin, a crumpled lottery ticket. All key elements
(text and both drawings) sit in the central horizontal strip of the image, away
from top and bottom edges.

### Памятка по загрузке
- Генерировать/экспортировать 2560x1440 (если генератор даёт 16:9 — это оно).
- Всё важное должно попасть в центральную зону 1546x423 (на телефонах видна только она) — поэтому промт держит текст и персонажей в центральной полосе.
- После загрузки проверить предпросмотр «ТВ / десктоп / мобильный» в YouTube Studio.

## ОПИСАНИЕ КАНАЛА (для страницы "О канале")

Money stories, badly drawn. Stickman documentaries about lottery winners who lost
everything, traders who beat Wall Street, scams that fooled entire countries, and
why your brain is terrible with money. Real data, real court records, real studies.
One crudely drawn video at a time. New episode every week.

## БОНУС: промт для аватарки канала (800x800, круглая обрезка)

Extremely simple amateur MS Paint style drawing. Square format. White background.
One stick figure head and shoulders, round head, dot eyes, a slightly crooked
confident smile, wearing a tiny hand-drawn crown made of dollar signs. Thick
uneven shaky black outlines, flat colors, no shading, no 3D. The face is large
and centered so it stays visible inside a circular crop. No text.
