## DESCRIPTION

He won $314.9 million on Christmas morning. Four years later he was begging to get his old life back.

Jack Whittaker, Bud Post, Evelyn Adams, Abraham Shakespeare. Different states, different decades, the same ending. This video breaks down why the luckiest people in America keep ending up broke, sued, and sometimes worse, using real bankruptcy data, a 1978 psychology study that compared lottery winners with accident victims, and the stories of winners who lost everything.
